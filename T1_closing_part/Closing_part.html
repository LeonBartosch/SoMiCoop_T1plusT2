Thank you for participating in the first part of this study.

This part assesses general individual differences (for instance self-reports on your personality) that we measure to investigate how they relate to individual behavioral measures that will be collected in the second part of the study.

The second part of the study will take place in the laboratory (SSH labs Sylvius building; Sylviusweg 72, 2333 BE Leiden). You have already registered for an appointment here. Please note that you will receive your payment only after having completed both parts of the study. It is thus vital that you take part in the second, laboratory part of the study.
If you have any questions or complaints about your privacy or the processing of your personal data, you can contact the privacy officer of Leiden University: privacy@bb.leidenuniv.nl.

Thank you for supporting our research!
